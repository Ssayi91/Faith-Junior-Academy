Admission Requirements

Everything you need to prepare for a successful application

Age Requirements
Required Documents
Assessment Criteria

Pre-Primary 1

  • Child must be 4 years old by 1st January of admission year
  • Basic self-help skills (toileting, feeding)
  • Ability to interact with peers in a group setting
  • No prior formal education required

Note: Children turning 4 between January and March may be considered on case-by-case basis.

Pre-Primary 2

  • Child must be 5 years old by 1st January of admission year
  • Completion of PP1 or equivalent preparation
  • Basic letter and number recognition
  • Ability to follow simple instructions

Note: Transfer students from other systems will be assessed for readiness.

Grades 1-3

  • Age-appropriate placement based on CBC progression
  • Successful completion of previous grade level
  • School reports from previous institution
  • Assessment of competency development

Note: We accept transfers from both CBC and 8-4-4 systems with appropriate bridging.

Identification Documents

  • Copy of child's birth certificate
  • Copy of child's immunization card
  • 4 recent passport-sized photographs
  • Copies of parents' national IDs
  • Transfer letter (for Grade 1-3 applicants)

Academic Records

  • Completed application form (online or physical)
  • Previous school reports (if applicable)
  • Recommendation letter from previous school (Grade 1-3)
  • CBC assessment portfolio (if transferring within CBC)

Medical & Consent Forms

  • Medical form completed by pediatrician
  • Consent for emergency medical treatment
  • Media release consent form
  • Transportation consent (if using school transport)
  • Parental agreement to school policies

Social & Emotional

  • Ability to separate from parents
  • Basic conflict resolution skills
  • Emotional regulation and expression
  • Cooperation in group activities
  • Following classroom routines

Cognitive Readiness

  • Age-appropriate attention span
  • Curiosity and question-asking
  • Problem-solving approaches
  • Memory and recall abilities
  • Following multi-step instructions

Language & Communication

  • Verbal communication skills
  • Listening comprehension
  • Basic vocabulary in English/Kiswahili
  • Expressing needs and ideas
  • Non-verbal communication understanding
